ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Key features of the organization

Accessibility

As a registered Canadian charitable organization and non-profit, TACK Canada is committed to accessibility. At TACK Canada, we strive to make educational opportunities, training, and leadership accessible to all.

Passion

We are passionate about supporting the youth of Canada, and we are committed to helping them reach their full potential. We focus on incorporating passion, fun, and growth into all of our work.

Opportunities

We organize events for high school students in Ontario and across Canada. These events include variety shows, tournaments, hackathons, and more, all designed to help students network whilst growing together.

Support

We value our community. We are committed to supporting our members, and making their time with TACK Canada as satisfactory as can be. Our support services range from local chapters, live assistants, to national executives and management personnel.
